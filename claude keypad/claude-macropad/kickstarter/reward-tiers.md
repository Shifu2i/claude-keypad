## Estimated Delivery Date Guidance

Per Kickstarter's own data, **84% of top hardware projects shipped late**.

- Take your internal best-case estimate and add **2–3 months buffer**.
- Never promise a date you cannot hit if everything goes slightly wrong.
- Communicate delays proactively — monthly backer updates are the minimum expected cadence.
